# Department of Extremely Important Research — Annual Report (Excerpt)

> In-universe document. Ref: `DEIR-AR-CURRENT`.

## Findings Concluded This Year

The Department concluded 214 findings this year, a figure consistent with prior years and not, the Department wishes to note, a decline.

## Notable Non-Findings

Three studies concluded without a publishable result. Per policy, these are not considered failures, but "findings indicating further findings are required." All three have been resubmitted for the following fiscal year.

## The Filing Cabinet

Remains unopened. Jointly certified by both parties as such. See `filing-cabinet-1974`.

## Peer Review Availability

Reviewers were unavailable for comment on 31 occasions this year, a slight increase attributed to reviewers being asleep, per Finding `DEIR-RF-0001`.

## Budget

Further funding has been requested. The Department reminds readers that this line has appeared, unchanged, in every annual report since 1957, and considers its consistency a finding in its own right.

## Closing Statement

The Department remains satisfied that its methodology, this year as in all prior years, held.

— Reviewed and Concluded By: Research Staff (names withheld pending peer review)
