# Office of Public Clarifications — Complaint and Response (Selected)

> In-universe document. Ref: `OPC-CORR-LOG`.

**Complaint received:**

"I have submitted four requests this year asking what, specifically, your office clarifies. I have received four responses. None have answered the question. Please clarify what you do."

**Response filed (`OPC-NC-0215`):**

"Your inquiry has been received and logged. Further clarification is not available at this time."

---

**Complaint received:**

"Regarding the red string donated to the Lost & Found Authority in 2015 — surely someone there knows something."

**Response filed (`OPC-NC-0089`, cross-ref `red-string-donation`):**

"This office is aware of the item in question. Further clarification is not available at this time."

---

**Internal memo (not for public release), Ref `OPC-MEMO-0004`:**

Staff are reminded that "further clarification is not available at this time" is the office's sole permitted response format, regardless of how the inquiry is phrased, how many times it has been asked, or how reasonable the request. Deviating from this format, even to say "we don't know," constitutes an unauthorized clarification and will be treated as such.
