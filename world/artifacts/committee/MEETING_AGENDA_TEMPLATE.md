# Committee for Poor Decisions — Standing Meeting Agenda Template

> In-universe document. Ref: `CPD-TEMPLATE-AGENDA`. Used, without variation, at every meeting since 1987.

1. **Call to Order.** Attendance recorded collectively as "the Committee," regardless of how many members are present.
2. **Approval of Previous Approved Outcome.** The prior meeting's decision is re-approved as read. It has never once been amended at this stage.
3. **Old Business.** Any item carried over from a previous meeting for lack of resolution. Some items have been "Old Business" since 1991.
4. **New Business.** New matters are introduced, discussed briefly, and, per §5, resolved.
5. **Motion and Vote.** A motion is proposed. It is seconded. It passes unanimously. This has occurred at every recorded meeting without exception, including the meeting a goose attended without objecting (`goose-incident`).
6. **Subcommittee Reports.** The Subcommittee on Office Supplies reports that no further staplers have relocated. This has been the entirety of its report since 1988.
7. **Any Other Business.** Historically, this is where most Committee decisions the Department later peer-reviews originate.
8. **Adjournment.** The meeting is adjourned. The Committee reconvenes per its standing schedule, having, once again, not discussed dissolving itself.

## Standing Rule

No agenda item may be tabled indefinitely without being reclassified as "Old Business," which functions, in practice, as the same thing under a different heading.
