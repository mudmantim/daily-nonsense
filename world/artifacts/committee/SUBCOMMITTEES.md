# Committee for Poor Decisions — Subcommittee Directory

> In-universe document. Ref: `CPD-DIR-SUBCOMMITTEES`.

- **Subcommittee on Office Supplies** — convened 1988 following the Great Stapler Migration (`stapler-migration`). Meets monthly. Standing report: no further staplers have relocated. Has not been formally asked to disband; would not comply if asked.
- **Subcommittee on Precedent** — maintains the Committee's citation list, including the Duck Harbor Master Precedent (`duck-harbor-master`) and the Tuesday Compromise (`tuesday-compromise`). Cited more often than it meets.
- **Subcommittee on Dissolution** — formed in 1990 to explore dissolving the Committee. Has met annually since, always inconclusively, and is, per its own charter, unable to dissolve itself without a Committee vote — which would require the Committee to remain in session to hold it.
- **Subcommittee on Wildlife Incidents** — formed after the Goose Incident (`goose-incident`) to liaise with Neighborhood Wildlife Reports. Has handled one (1) incident to date.

## Note on Quorum

Quorum requires a majority of Committee members present, "present" being interpreted broadly enough that a goose has, on at least one occasion, satisfied it (see `goose-incident`). The Committee has never revisited this definition.
