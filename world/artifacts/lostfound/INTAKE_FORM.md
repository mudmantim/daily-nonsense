# Lost & Found Authority — Item Intake Form

> In-universe document. Ref: `LFA-FORM-INTAKE`.

```
ITEM INTAKE
Reference: LFA-[####]
Item description: [_____]
Condition: [_____ — note: "lost" and "unclaimed" are not valid entries; use "awaiting recognition"]
Believed connected to: [optional — see TIMELINE.md]
Claim window: Optimistically Ongoing
```

## Filed Examples

`LFA-0205` — One left sock. Condition: emotionally prepared. Cross-reference: `sock-inquiry`.
`LFA-0310` — One jar, labeled DO NOT OPEN — SERIOUSLY. Condition: unopened, six years running.
`LFA-1815` — One length of red string. Origin unknown. Believed connected to: nothing on file. The Office of Public Clarifications was asked; see `red-string-donation`.

## Standing Rule

No item is ever marked "disposed of." The Authority has no procedure for disposal, on the theory that an item disposed of might, in principle, still be recognized by someone, someday, and disposal would foreclose that possibility. This is treated as sound custodial practice, not as a hoarding problem.
