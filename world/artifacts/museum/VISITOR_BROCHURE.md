# The Museum of Useless History — Visitor Brochure

> In-universe document. Ref: `MUH-BR-0001`.

**Welcome.**

The Museum of Useless History preserves what history did not ask to be preserved. Every exhibit is historically underappreciated, and we intend to keep it that way.

**Hours:** Open Tuesdays. Closed during dramatic pauses.

**Highlights:**
- *Artifact #442 — Wooden Spoon.* Estimated age: Tuesday.
- *The Treaty of Overly Specific Terms (1801).* Nine pages, concerning one goat. Context pending.
- *The Jointly-Held Filing Cabinet.* Co-owned with the Department of Extremely Important Research since 1974. Not on display. Please do not ask to see inside; the answer, since 1974, has not changed.

**Gift Shop:** Sells postcards of exhibits that are, per policy, too historically underappreciated to photograph well.

**A Note From the Curator:** Every object here mattered to someone, once, or matters to me, now, which the Museum considers equivalent.
